OUR STORY

A TOP-RATED BOAT
DEALERSHIP

Tilly's Marine started in Riverside, California with one goal: provide an exceptional customer experience before, during, and after every purchase. Four decades later, that promise continues to guide everything we do.

GROWING WITH SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA

From one dealership to three convenient locations, we've expanded while staying true to our family-owned values. Every showroom is staffed by boating enthusiasts who know the products and genuinely care about helping customers enjoy more time on the water.

WHAT WE SELL, WE SERVICE

Whether you're purchasing your first boat or upgrading your current one, our certified technicians, financing experts and customer care team are committed to keeping you on the water for years to come.

BUILT FOR BOATERS

BUILT BY BOATERS

Tilly's team isn't just selling boats—we live the boating lifestyle ourselves. From wake surfing to relaxing cruises, we understand exactly what makes time on the water unforgettable.
